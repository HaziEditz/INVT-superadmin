Typecheck is clean across the board. All three bugs are fixed. Here's a summary of what was found and corrected:

Bug 1 — Food & Freight screens: no payment option appeared selected on load
Both screens initialised payment as "cash", which is no longer shown in the PaymentSelector. The first render would display four options with none highlighted. Fixed by defaulting to "card".

Bug 2 — Ride-complete screen: payment method lost in history for new types
When writing a completed trip to Firestore history, the code only branched on "wallet" and "cash", mapping every other method — including "account", "business_account", "acc", and "gift_card" — to "card". History records for those payment types were silently misclassified. Fixed with a full mapping: business-account variants → "account", gift card → "gift_card", everything else → "card".

Bug 3 — Cancel policy: gift card bookings got wrong cancellation outcome
computeCancelPolicy only included "card" and "wallet" in willRefund. A gift card booking cancelled within the grace window would get outcome "free" (no refund) instead of "refund" (credit returned to wallet), which is wrong — the passenger paid real value. Fixed by adding "gift_card" to the refundable set.

No other runtime bugs were found — auth, active-ride tracking, scheduled rides, towing, rental, SOS, and the chat modal all look structurally sound. The typecheck remains clean.
