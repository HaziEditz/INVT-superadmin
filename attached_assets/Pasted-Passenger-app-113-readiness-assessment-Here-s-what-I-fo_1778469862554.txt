What I can build right now (no Storage rules needed):

Photo capture mode — swap the barcode CameraView for ImagePicker.launchCameraAsync with a card-shaped overlay. Photo is held in local state and shown as a thumbnail. No upload yet — the URL field stays empty until Storage rules land.
Profile persistence — write the TM card (number, name, expiry, hoist flag, cardCaptureMethod) to users/{uid}/tmCard in RTDB on save. Read it back in the booking screen on mount and pre-fill tmPassengers automatically.
"Change" link — in the TM card row on the booking screen, when the card came from the profile, show "Saved card — Change" instead of "Add your TM card".
cardCaptureMethod — add to booking RTDB/Firestore writes ("camera" or "manual").
Account/ACC — add an optional "Take card photo" button using ImagePicker (thumbnail shown inline, no upload).
What waits for Storage rules:

Uploading the photo and writing tmCardPhotoUrl / accountCardPhotoUrl / accCardPhotoUrl to booking records. That's a one-function addition once you get the all-clear.
One question before starting:

The spec says "save to profile, auto-fill on future bookings." For a TM card that's saved to users/{uid}/tmCard, should the booking screen auto-add it to the tmPassengers list when TM mode is toggled on — or should it auto-fill the scanner and let the passenger confirm before adding? Confirming first is safer (card might have expired), but auto-adding is smoother if the registry lookup happens on mount.
