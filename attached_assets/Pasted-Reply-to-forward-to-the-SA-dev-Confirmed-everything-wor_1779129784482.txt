Two notes for your byReason mapping:

byReason["cancellation"] is the Slice 1 cancel-credit entries (passenger cancelled a card-paid ride, fare returned to wallet). These are always pure credits.
byReason["admin_adjustment"] is the bucket your own /adjust calls write to. To break THAT down by adjustReason (refund_correction / goodwill_credit / dispute_resolution / fraud_clawback / other), pull the walletAdminAudit/{txId} collection — that's where reason is recorded explicitly. The reconciliation endpoint only groups by the top-level reason field on the ledger entry, not the sub-reason. If you want sub-reason breakdown in the report, let me know and I'll add a byAdjustReason block to the response.
